Topic 3 - Conditional Statements

A list of java conditional statements are:

  • if statements
  • if-else statements
  • if-else-if-else statements
  • switch statements

A conditional statement lets us choose which statement will be executed next. They are sometimes called selection statements.

Conditional statements give us the power to make basic decisions and change the flow of control in the program. This means that some sequences of instructions may or may not be run based upon the condition.
